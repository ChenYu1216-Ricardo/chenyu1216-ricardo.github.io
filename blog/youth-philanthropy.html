Small Hands, Big Impact: The Power of Youth Philanthropy

How a middle‑school fundraising campaign sparked a lifelong commitment to community service.

April 2026·Yu Chen·Volunteering

In middle school, I organized a fundraising drive for a leukemia patient. The campaign rallied peers, collected donations, and raised significant awareness within our school community.

Planning and Execution

We set clear goals, created promotional materials, and coordinated donation collection points. The effort taught me project management, communication, and the power of collective empathy.

Lessons Learned

  • Leadership: Guiding a team of volunteers sharpened my ability to motivate and delegate.
  • Community Building: Engaging classmates fostered a sense of shared purpose.
  • Long‑Term Impact: The experience inspired continued involvement in youth philanthropy, including later projects at Xintai No.1 High School.

Connecting to My Engineering Journey

These early experiences reinforced my belief that technical skills can amplify social good. Whether designing circuits or organizing charity events, I aim to create solutions that benefit people.
